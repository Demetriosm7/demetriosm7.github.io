Who Am I?

I'm Demetrios Michael, a web designer/developer based in the Charlotte, North Carolina area of the United States.

I enjoy building websites, using a mobile-first approach, that can help bring people and businesses together.

Design

There is nothing more satisfying then helping people and businesss bring their dreams to life. I work closely with clients to gain a clear understanding of how they want their websites to look and feel in order to convey the message that they want to send.

Develop

As a designer/developer, I understand the tools necessary to get the job done. It’s important to me, when creating websites, that they run cleanly across multiple devices using the latest technologies along with a mobile-first approach.

Deploy

I take pride in the work that I create ensuring that the final product is fully functional, user-friendly and responsive. One of the most exciting things to see is your idea in action.

Front-End Skill Set

I've been working on front-end design using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. I also have a knowledge of ReactJS, jQuery, Version Control/Git, Wireframing, Bootstrap and Photoshop. I focus on simplistic, clean and responsive web designs. I’m always expanding my knowledge of web technologies in order to enhance my techniques.

Back-End Skill Set

I’m also applying myself in the back-end of development in areas such as PHP, MongoDB, NodeJS, JSON and API architectures (REST/SOAP) so that I can become a more rounded web developer.

Why Web Development?

I have a strong passion for web design because it gives me the freedom, creativity and flexibility to produce wonderful and artistic platforms that help connect people. It's an ever-evolving field that offers many rewards.
